My Decks

Below are decks I've ran and had success with. They are not going to be as perfected as some of the decks that you see S-Rank players, but these are decks I've climbed with.

  1. Vetruvian pseud-budget. Ran this deck from 30 (started this month) to around 16.
  2. Vetruvian Midrange 2. Ran this from 16 to around 11 and then my win rate wasn't as good.
  3. Lyonar Counter. This deck is heavy taunt and dispel oriented - tends to counter well a lot of the meta right now. Used this from around 12 - 10.
  4. Creepy Cassyva. This deck is focused on creep combos. It's a more expensive deck and not perfected, but I got a decent winrate with it.
